Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all. Our beginner programs are designed for absolute novices. Our masters will guide you through the fundamental movements of Silambam, Karate, or Kickboxing at a pace that suits your current ability.

What is the minimum age for enrollment?

We offer "Youth Warrior" classes for children aged 5 and above. For our traditional and combat adult programs, we typically start from age 14.

What should I wear for my first class?

For your trial session, comfortable athletic wear (T-shirt and track pants) is perfect. Once you enroll, we will provide guidance on the specific uniform (Dobok/Gi) or safety gear required for your chosen discipline.

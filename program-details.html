Detailed Syllabus

The Mastery Paths.

01

Silambam Mastery

Silambam is an ancient weapon-based martial art from Tamil Nadu, focusing primarily on the bamboo staff. This program is designed to transform a student’s coordination by mastering rotational momentum. Students begin with basic spins (Sujatru) and progress toward complex combat applications and (Chuvadu) that engage the entire body.

  • Ambidextrous Handling
  • Wrist Flexibility
  • Defensive Blocking
  • Rhythmic Footwork
02

Traditional Karate

Our Karate program is rooted in the Shotokan tradition, emphasizing deep, stable stances and the explosive generation of linear power. The curriculum balances three pillars: Kihon (basics), Kata (forms), and Kumite (sparring). It is a journey of "Ikken Hisatsu"—to finish a strike with absolute precision and focus while maintaining perfect mental calm.

  • Stance Stability
  • Hip Power Rotation
  • Precise Striking
  • Mental Fortitude
03

Olympic Taekwondo

Recognized globally as an Olympic sport, Taekwondo focuses on dynamic, high-speed kicking and agile movement. This program integrates traditional discipline with modern sports science. Students learn to master the Electronic Point Scoring System (PSS) used in international tournaments, emphasizing speed, accuracy, and tactical.

  • Kicking Speed
  • Agility & Footwork
  • PSS Scoring Logic
  • Core Flexibility
04

Tactical Defense

This is a pragmatic, no-nonsense self-defense system designed for real-world scenarios. It combines the joint-locking efficiency of Hapkido with the direct striking of Krav Maga. The curriculum removes the "sport" element to focus purely on situational awareness, threat de-escalation, and high-pressure survival techniques for urban environments.

  • Joint Manipulation
  • Threat Neutralization
  • Stress Management
  • Close Quarters Combat
